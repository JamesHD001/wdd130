Our History

Founded in 1998, we began as a small team with a big dream — to bring the joy of rafting to everyone. Today, we've grown into one of the most trusted names in outdoor adventure travel.

Our journey has been one of passion, commitment, and exploration. We've navigated rivers across continents and shared our love for the water with thrill-seekers of all ages.
